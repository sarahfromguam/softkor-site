jeffry ejan

the artist behind softkor

Jeff Ejan is the artist behind it all. A Guam native, he draws on his island upbringing, indigenous Filipino heritage, and mystical experiences in his art. His experience is extensive, ranging from his expressive, stylistic characters to his iconic murals to his one-of-a-kind paintings. His tattoos, which he creates under the alias of "stickybirtd", reflect his deep understanding of art across almost every medium out there. It comes as no surprise that he is commonly referred to as one of the best artists on island.
